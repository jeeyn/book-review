## CHAPTER 1 : WHERE ARE WE IN THE SIX STAGES?
- First Epoch : Birth of the laws of physics and chemistry
	- We live in a universe that is very precisely balanced. If it was slightly off we would not be here. 
- Second Epoch : Life. Living creatures evolves and spreads
- Third Epoch : Animals formed brains which stores and process information 
- Fourth Epoch : Animals used high level cognitive ability to translate thoughts into complex actions(=Humans)
	- Humans created technology that was able to store and manipulate information from papyrus to hard drives
	- These tech augmented our ability to process infomation patterns
- Fifth Epoch : Merge human cognition with technology (=Brain-computer interfaces)
	- Humans will connect neocortices to the cloud to extend our thinking. AI would be an extension of ourselves rather than a competitor
- Sixth Epoch : intelligence spreads throughout the universe (matters turn into computronium)

- We are now in the Fourth Epoch.
- AI will pass the Turing test by 2029 and that would bring us to the Fifth Epoch.
	- Turing Test measures measures human-level indistinguishability so a machine would have to dumb itself down or simulate human flaws to convince a judge it is a person.
